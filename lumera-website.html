FAQ

Questions, answered.

What happens if a candidate refuses to install the agent?
+
Lumera falls back to browser-only monitoring via WebRTC, which still provides strong detection for behavioral signals and external voice. However, desktop agent install is recommended for complete coverage — most companies make it a pre-interview requirement.
What data does Lumera collect?
+
Only interview-session data — process names, Bluetooth device names (not content), network hostnames, and behavioral signals from the camera/microphone. We never record personal files, browsing history, or keystrokes. Full details in our privacy policy.
Is the agent removed after the interview?
+
Yes. The agent terminates automatically when the interview ends and can be uninstalled with one click. No residual processes, background services, or telemetry run after the session.
Does Lumera work on Mac and Linux?
+
Yes. Lumera supports Windows 10/11, macOS 12+, and Ubuntu 22.04+. The same detection capabilities work across all three platforms.
How accurate is the detection?
+
Our current detection accuracy is 97%+ across all fraud vectors, with a false positive rate under 2%. We continuously retrain our models on adversarially generated data to stay ahead of new cheating tactics.
Can Lumera integrate with our ATS?
+
Yes. Native integrations with Naukri, Zoho Recruit, Greenhouse, Lever, and Workday. Fraud reports push directly into candidate profiles. Custom integrations available on enterprise plans.
How much does Lumera cost?
+
Starter plan begins at ₹999/month for 10 interviews. Growth plan at ₹9,999/month for 300 interviews. Custom enterprise pricing available. Compared to Sherlock (₹8,300+/month) and Talview (₹1.75L+/month), Lumera is 80% more affordable.
